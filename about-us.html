Toronto Can-Musician Education Conservatory

is a music school locates in Toronto, the largest city in Canada with many museums, theatres, and festival events, we are offering online/in-person instrumental and vocal learning lessons that try to build a platform for musicians and music lovers to communicate with each other.

Our classes include piano, strings, winds, vocal coaching, and music theory from elementary through advanced levels. In addition, we also offer introduction to composition, assemble and chamber music for youth that particularly designed to develop interest and gain experience in stage performance.

Our teachers are music professionals who are really experienced in teaching different types of instruments. From time to time, we invite a Juno Award nominee to offer Master Class for our students who come from many different locations for all ages.

Whether you are a beginner or advanced learner, in TCEC, you are not only learning play the instrument, but also a culture.
